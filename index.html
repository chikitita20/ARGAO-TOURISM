Top Tourist Destinations

Explore the most beautiful and exciting places Argao has to offer

Bugasok Falls

A magnificent multi-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush tropical vegetation. Perfect for swimming and nature photography.

Liki Spring

A refreshing natural spring with crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming and relaxation in a pristine environment.

St. Michael the Archangel Church

A historic Spanish colonial church built in the 16th century, showcasing beautiful architecture and religious heritage.

Mahayahay Beach

A hidden gem with untouched natural beauty, ideal for camping, fishing, and watching spectacular sunsets.

Ka Treasure Water Terraces

Stunning agricultural terraces with natural spring irrigation, showcasing traditional farming and breathtaking landscapes.

About Argao

Argao is a coastal municipality in the province of Cebu, Philippines, known for its rich history, natural attractions, and warm hospitality. From cascading waterfalls to pristine beaches, ancient churches to natural springs, Argao offers a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility for every traveler.
