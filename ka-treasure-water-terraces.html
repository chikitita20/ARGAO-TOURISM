Ka Treasure Water Terraces

Discover nature’s hidden gem at Ka Treasure Water Terraces — where crystal-clear spring waters flow through natural limestone steps, offering a refreshing escape in the heart of Argao’s lush mountains.

About Ka Treasure Water Terraces

The natural charm of Ka Treasure Water Terraces lies in its lush greenery and serene atmosphere. Crystal-clear spring waters cascade gently over natural rock formations, creating terraces that are as visually stunning as they are soothing to the soul. by thriving flora, the terraces offer a refreshing escape, where the cool air and tranquil sounds of flowing water invite visitors to relax and reconnect with nature. It's the perfect retreat for those seeking peace away from the stresses of daily life. Conveniently located in Catang, Argao, Ka Treasure is easily accessible, yet feels like a hidden paradise — far removed from the noise and rush of the city. Many visitors leave not only refreshed but also inspired by the raw beauty and stillness of this mountain sanctuary.

How to Get There

From Cebu City: Take a bus to Argao town (2-3 hours), then hire a tricycle or habal-habal to the terraces (45 minutes through mountain roads).

By Private Vehicle: Follow mountain roads from Argao town center. 4WD recommended for steep and winding terrain leading to the terraces.

Tips: Start early morning for best lighting and cooler weather. Wear comfortable walking shoes for uneven terrain. Consider hiring a local guide to learn about traditional farming practices and respect the working agricultural areas.
