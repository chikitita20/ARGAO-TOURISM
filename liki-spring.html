Liki Spring

A refreshing natural spring with crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming and relaxation in a pristine environment.

About Liki Spring

Liki Spring is a hidden natural gem in Argao, featuring crystal-clear spring waters that maintain a refreshingly cool temperature year-round. This pristine spring is surrounded by lush tropical vegetation, creating a tranquil oasis perfect for swimming, relaxation, and connecting with nature.

The spring's pure, clean waters flow continuously from underground sources, creating a natural swimming pool that remains clear and inviting. The peaceful environment and natural beauty make Liki Spring an ideal destination for families and nature lovers seeking a refreshing escape.

Operating Hours

Open 24 hours (Best visited during daylight hours for safety)

How to Get There

From Cebu City: Take a bus to Argao town (2-3 hours), then hire a tricycle or habal-habal to Liki Spring (25 minutes through local roads).

By Private Vehicle: Drive to Argao town center, then follow local roads with signage to Liki Spring. Parking is available near the spring.

Tips: Bring swimwear and towels for the refreshing spring waters. The area has basic facilities, so consider bringing snacks and drinks for your visit.
