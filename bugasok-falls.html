Bugasok Falls

A magnificent multi-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush tropical vegetation, offering a perfect escape into nature's paradise.

About Bugasok Falls

Bugasok Falls is a scenic natural attraction located in Barangay Conalum, Argao, Cebu. Its cool, cascading waters flow over rugged limestone formations into a refreshing catch basin surrounded by lush forest. The area’s serene atmosphere makes it perfect for swimming, sightseeing, and nature photography.

Known for its unspoiled charm, Bugasok Falls is home to various local plants and wildlife, adding to its natural appeal. It remains one of Argao’s hidden gems and offers a mix of relaxation and adventure—ideal for families, backpackers, and thrill-seekers looking for a peaceful yet exciting escape.


Location and Access

Bugasok Falls is accessible via habal-habal (motorbike), jeepney, or private vehicle from Argao town proper. From the drop-off point, visitors typically follow a short trail or ride a bamboo raft depending on water conditions. The journey adds to the adventure and provides scenic views of the river and surrounding greenery.


Safety and Environmental Reminder

To preserve the beauty of the falls, guests are encouraged to:

  • Follow guide instructions and safety rules
  • Wear proper footwear for trekking
  • Avoid littering, bring your trash with you
  • Respect wildlife and local surroundings
  • Check weather and water conditions before visiting

Operating Hours

Open daily: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Daytime visits are advised for safety, accessibility, and coordination with local guides.

How to Get There

From Cebu City: Take a bus to Argao town (2-3 hours). From Argao town center, hire a tricycle or habal-habal to Barangay Bugasok (30 minutes).

Trek: From the drop-off point, follow the forest trail for approximately 30 minutes to reach the falls.

Tips: Wear comfortable hiking shoes and bring water. Local guides are available for hire in the barangay.
